Record types Record types

National Records

National records are published by Governments and include national information relevant for the implementation of the Nagoya Protocol as well as information Parties are obliged to provide in accordance with the Protocol.

  • ABS National Focal Points
  • Competent National Authorities
  • ABS Procedures
  • Legislative, Administrative or Policy Measures
  • National Model Contractual Clauses
  • Internationally Recognized Certificates of Compliance
  • National Websites or Databases
  • Checkpoints
  • Checkpoint Communiqués
  • National Reports
